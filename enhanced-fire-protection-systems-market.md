<p><p>The Enhanced Fire Protection Systems market consists of two primary types: active systems and passive systems. Active systems include devices such as sprinklers, fire alarms, and extinguishers, designed to detect and actively suppress fires upon activation. In contrast, passive systems encompass architectural elements like fire-resistant walls, doors, and materials that aim to contain fires and hinder their spread without active intervention. Together, these systems enhance overall fire safety and risk management in various environments, ensuring better protection for lives and property.</p></p>
