<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow</li><li>Agglutination Assays</li><li>Flow-Through</li><li>Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Medical Diagnostic Devices market comprises various types, including lateral flow assays, which use capillary action for quick results, and agglutination assays, which rely on the clumping of particles to detect substances. Flow-through assays utilize a similar principle but allow a sample to flow through a membrane, enhancing sensitivity. Biosensors combine biological components with electronic systems for real-time analysis. Together, these technologies enable swift diagnosis and monitoring of diseases, improving patient outcomes and healthcare efficiency.</p></p>
