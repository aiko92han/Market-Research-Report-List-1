<p><ul><li>Single In-line Conductivity Meter</li><li>Dual In-line Conductivity Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line conductivity meters are crucial for measuring the conductivity of liquids in real-time within processing systems. The single in-line conductivity meter monitors one flow stream, providing straightforward conductivity readings for consistent process control. In contrast, the dual in-line conductivity meter evaluates two flow streams simultaneously, allowing for comparative analysis and enhanced monitoring of complex systems. Both types are essential in industries like water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, ensuring optimal quality and regulatory compliance in liquid management.</p></p>
