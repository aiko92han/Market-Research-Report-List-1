<p><p>The genitourinary prosthetics market comprises three main types: silicone, titanium, and apatite. Silicone prosthetics are favored for their flexibility and biocompatibility, providing a realistic feel and appearance. Titanium prosthetics are known for their durability and strength, making them suitable for long-term implantation. Apatite-based prosthetics offer excellent osteoconductivity, promoting bone integration and enhancing stability. Together, these materials cater to diverse patient needs and preferences in reconstructive urology and related medical fields.</p></p>
