<p><p>The handheld electric muscle stimulator market includes several types of stimulation technologies. Neuromuscular electrical stimulation (NMES) targets muscle activation for rehabilitation and strength building. Functional electrical stimulation (FES) is used to restore function in individuals with impaired motor control. Transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS) alleviates pain by stimulating nerves. Burst mode alternating current delivers pulsed currents for muscle relaxation. Microcurrent electrical neuro muscular stimulation (MENS) uses low-level currents to enhance muscle recovery. Other variations may include specialized devices and techniques.</p></p>
