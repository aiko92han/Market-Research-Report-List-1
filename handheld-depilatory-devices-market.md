<p><p>Handheld depilatory devices utilize two primary technologies: Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) and laser. In contrast, laser devices focus a single wavelength of light on specific hair colors, providing precise and effective hair removal. Both technologies offer convenience and portability, allowing users to achieve salon-like results at home, meeting the growing demand for personal grooming solutions.</p></p>
