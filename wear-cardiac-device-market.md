<p><p>The Wear Cardiac Device Market encompasses various types designed to monitor and manage heart health. Defibrillators are lifesaving devices that deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm during arrhythmias. Patch monitors are wearable devices that continuously track heart activity, providing real-time data to both patients and healthcare providers. Holter monitors are portable electrocardiograms worn for 24 hours or more, capturing heart rhythms to diagnose irregularities. Together, these devices enhance proactive cardiac care and patient insights.</p></p>
