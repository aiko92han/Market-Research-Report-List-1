<p><strong>The Residential Smart Smoke Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential smart smoke detector market comprises three main types: photoelectric, dual sensor, and ionization detectors. Photoelectric detectors use a light beam and sensor to detect smoldering fires, reducing false alarms from cooking. Dual sensor models combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies for comprehensive detection of fast-flaming and slow-smoldering fires. Ionization detectors rely on radioactive material to sense flames quickly but may trigger false alarms more often. Each type offers unique advantages for enhancing home safety and fire response.</p></p>
