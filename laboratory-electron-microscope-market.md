<p><strong>The Laboratory Electron Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SEM</li><li>TEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Electron Microscope market includes two primary types: Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM) and Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM). SEM is used for high-resolution imaging of surface structures by scanning samples with focused electron beams, providing detailed 3D views. TEM, on the other hand, allows electron transmission through ultra-thin samples to produce images at atomic resolution, ideal for analyzing internal structures. Both technologies are vital for advancements in materials science, nanotechnology, and biological research, driving growth in the market.</p></p>
