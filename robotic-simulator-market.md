<p><strong>The Robotic Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-premises</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic simulator market is divided into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises simulators are installed locally on a user’s hardware, providing control over the environment and data security but requiring a significant initial investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based simulators offer flexibility and accessibility by allowing users to access simulations remotely through the internet, often on a subscription basis, thus reducing hardware costs and enabling easier updates and collaboration among users.</p></p>
