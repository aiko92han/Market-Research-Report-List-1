<p><strong>The Automotive Air Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capsule Air Spring</li><li>Membrane Air Spring</li><li>Compound Air Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air spring market comprises three main types: capsule air springs, membrane air springs, and compound air springs. Capsule air springs feature a simple design, offering basic load support and comfort. Membrane air springs utilize a flexible membrane for enhanced shock absorption and payload flexibility. Compound air springs combine the benefits of both capsule and membrane designs, providing superior performance and adaptability to varying loads and driving conditions, making them ideal for both commercial and passenger vehicles.</p></p>
