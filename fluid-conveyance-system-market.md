<p><p>The Fluid Conveyance System market is categorized into three main types: fuel-based, air-based, and hydraulic systems. Fuel-based systems use liquid fuels to transport fluids, commonly found in automotive and industrial applications. Air-based systems utilize compressed air to convey fluids, often employed in pneumatic controls and tools. Hydraulic systems rely on pressurized liquids to transmit power and control machinery, widely used in construction and manufacturing. Each type offers distinct operational benefits suited to various industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
