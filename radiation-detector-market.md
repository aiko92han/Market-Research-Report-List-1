<p><strong>The Radiation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Ionization Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation detector market comprises three main types: gas ionization detectors, semiconductor detectors, and scintillation detectors. Gas ionization detectors operate by ionizing gas molecules to measure radiation, making them effective for high-energy particles. Semiconductor detectors use solid materials to detect radiation by generating electron-hole pairs, offering high resolution and efficiency. Scintillation detectors utilize materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, enabling quick counting and energy measurement. Each type serves distinct applications in medical, industrial, and environmental monitoring sectors.</p></p>
