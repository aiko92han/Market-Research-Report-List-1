<p><p>The pesticide inert ingredients market is divided into two primary types: dry and liquid. Dry pesticide inert ingredients typically include powders or granules used to enhance delivery and effectiveness in application. They often serve as carriers, fillers, or stabilizers. In contrast, liquid pesticide inert ingredients are solutions or emulsions that facilitate blending and compatibility with active substances. Both types play crucial roles in improving the performance, stability, and usability of pesticide formulations, contributing to more efficient pest management.</p></p>
