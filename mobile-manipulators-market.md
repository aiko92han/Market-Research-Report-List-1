<p><p>The mobile manipulators market can be categorized into two main types: omnidirectional and non-omnidirectional. Omnidirectional mobile manipulators are designed to move in any direction, providing enhanced maneuverability and flexibility in navigating complex environments. This makes them ideal for tasks requiring precise positioning. Conversely, non-omnidirectional manipulators move primarily in linear paths, often utilizing wheels in fixed orientations. While they may offer greater stability for straightforward tasks, their limited movement can restrict versatility in dynamic settings.</p></p>
