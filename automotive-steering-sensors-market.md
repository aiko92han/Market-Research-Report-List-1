<p><ul><li>Contacting</li><li>Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering sensors market is primarily divided into two types: contacting and magnetic sensors. Contacting sensors utilize physical contact to measure steering position and angle, offering high accuracy but potentially experiencing wear over time. In contrast, magnetic sensors employ magnetic fields to gauge rotational movement without physical contact, providing durability and resistance to environmental factors. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and performance by enabling advanced driver assistance systems and improving steering responsiveness.</p></p>
