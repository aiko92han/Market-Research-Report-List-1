<p><p>Oral Polio Vaccines (OPV) are crucial for polio eradication efforts. The market features two primary types: Trivalent OPV (tOPV) and Bivalent OPV (bOPV). </p><p>tOPV contains all three poliovirus strains (type 1, 2, and 3) and has historically been used for comprehensive immunization. In contrast, bOPV includes only types 1 and 3, enhancing immunity against these more prevalent strains while eliminating the type 2 component to reduce vaccine-derived poliovirus risks. Each serves specific epidemiological needs in polio prevention.</p></p>
