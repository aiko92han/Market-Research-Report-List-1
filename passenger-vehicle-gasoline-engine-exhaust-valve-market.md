<p><p>The passenger vehicle gasoline engine exhaust valve market consists of three main types. Mono metallic valves are made from a single type of metal, offering good thermal stability and durability. Bimetallic valves combine two different metals, enhancing resistance to heat and wear, thus improving performance. Hollow valves are lightweight and feature a hollow structure, reducing weight while maintaining strength, which leads to better fuel efficiency and performance. Each type is designed to meet specific engine requirements and optimize performance.</p></p>
