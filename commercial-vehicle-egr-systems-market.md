<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle EGR Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric EGR Valve</li><li>Pneumatic EGR Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) Systems market includes two primary types: Electric EGR Valves and Pneumatic EGR Valves. Electric EGR Valves utilize electrical signals to regulate the flow of exhaust gases, offering precise control and faster response times, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. Conversely, Pneumatic EGR Valves operate using air pressure to manage exhaust recirculation, typically providing a simpler, more cost-effective solution. Both types play crucial roles in meeting strict environmental regulations and improving vehicle performance.</p></p>
