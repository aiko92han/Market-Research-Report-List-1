<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) market features three primary vehicle types. Tilt-X designs utilize tilting rotor systems for both vertical lift and horizontal flight, offering efficiency and speed. Lift+Cruise vehicles use dedicated lift rotors for takeoff and landing, transitioning to fixed wings for cruise, optimizing range and performance. Multi-copters rely on multiple rotors for vertical flight, allowing for simpler designs and greater maneuverability, though typically with limited range. Each type caters to different urban mobility needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
