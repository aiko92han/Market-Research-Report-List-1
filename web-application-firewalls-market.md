<p><p>The Web Application Firewalls (WAF) market is primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based WAFs are hosted off-site and offer scalability, ease of deployment, and lower upfront costs, making them suitable for businesses with fluctuating traffic. Conversely, on-premises WAFs are installed within a company's own infrastructure, providing greater control, customization, and compliance, ideal for organizations with stringent security requirements. Both types aim to safeguard web applications from various online threats.</p></p>
