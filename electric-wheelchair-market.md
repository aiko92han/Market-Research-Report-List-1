<p><strong>The Electric Wheelchair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li><li>Wet-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric wheelchair market is primarily divided into two types based on battery technology: dry-battery electric wheelchairs and wet-battery electric wheelchairs. Dry-battery electric wheelchairs utilize sealed lead-acid or lithium batteries, offering advantages such as lighter weight and reduced maintenance. Meanwhile, wet-battery electric wheelchairs rely on traditional liquid electrolyte batteries, typically providing longer operational times but requiring more regular upkeep. Each type caters to specific user needs, balancing factors like mobility, convenience, and battery life for optimal performance.</p></p>
