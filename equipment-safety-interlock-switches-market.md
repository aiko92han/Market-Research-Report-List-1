<p><ul><li>Magnetic-Force Interlocking</li><li>Spring-Force Interlocking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Equipment Safety Interlock Switches market is primarily segmented into two types: Magnetic-Force Interlocking and Spring-Force Interlocking. Magnetic-Force Interlocking utilizes magnetic force to ensure that machinery cannot operate unless specific safety conditions are met, enhancing operational safety. In contrast, Spring-Force Interlocking relies on mechanical springs to engage or disengage the switch, providing reliable operation and quicker response times. Both types play crucial roles in safeguarding equipment, preventing accidents, and ensuring compliance with safety regulations across various industries.</p></p>
