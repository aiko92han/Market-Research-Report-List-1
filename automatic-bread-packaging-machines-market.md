<p><p>The automatic bread packaging machines market can be categorized into three types: primary, secondary, and tertiary packaging. Primary packaging directly encloses the bread, such as bags or films that maintain freshness. Secondary packaging involves grouping primary packages for branding and handling, like boxes or cartons. Tertiary packaging provides bulk handling, facilitating storage and transportation, often using pallets or shrink wraps. Each type plays a crucial role in protecting the product and enhancing shelf appeal while ensuring efficient logistics.</p></p>
