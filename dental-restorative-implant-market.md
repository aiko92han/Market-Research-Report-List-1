In contrast, zirconia implants offer a tooth-like appearance, excellent aesthetics, and are metal-free, appealing to patients with metal sensitivities. Both types cater to diverse patient needs, balancing functionality with aesthetic preferences, while driving innovation and growth in the dental implant industry.</p></p>
