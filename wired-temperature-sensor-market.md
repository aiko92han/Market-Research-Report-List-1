<p><p>The wired temperature sensor market can be categorized into several types: One Wire, Two Wire, Three Wire, and Others. Two Wire sensors are cost-effective and commonly used, as they require only two connections, but may sacrifice some accuracy. Three Wire sensors offer enhanced accuracy and are ideal for industrial applications, ensuring better compensation for lead resistance.
