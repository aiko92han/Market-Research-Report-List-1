<p><p>The waterproof coating market is primarily divided into two types: dry and liquid coatings. Dry coatings often come in the form of powder or tape, providing a robust barrier when applied. They offer flexibility in coverage and can conform to various surfaces, making them suitable for intricate designs and irregular shapes.</p></p>
