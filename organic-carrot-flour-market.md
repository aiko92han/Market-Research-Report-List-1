<p><p>The organic carrot flour market features two primary types: spray-dried carrot powder and freeze-dried carrot powder. Spray-drying involves rapidly evaporating moisture from carrot puree, resulting in a fine powder that retains nutrients and flavor, ideal for a variety of food products. Freeze-drying, on the other hand, preserves the carrots' structure and nutritional content by freezing them and removing moisture under vacuum, creating a lightweight powder. Both methods cater to health-conscious consumers seeking natural, nutritious ingredients.</p></p>
