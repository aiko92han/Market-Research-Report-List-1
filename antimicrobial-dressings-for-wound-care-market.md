Honey antimicrobial dressings leverage honey's natural antibacterial properties, helping to maintain a moist environment. Polyhexamethylene biguanide (PHMB) antimicrobial dressings use PHMB to reduce microbial colonization and are suitable for chronic and acute wounds.</p></p>
