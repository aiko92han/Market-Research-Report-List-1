<p><p>Email management software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based email management software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access their email services from any location with internet connectivity. This type often offers scalability and automatic updates. On-premises solutions, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company's hardware.
