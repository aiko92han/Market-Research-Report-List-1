<p><strong>The Differential For Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Differential</li><li>Lock Differential</li><li>Limited Slip Differential</li><li>Electronic Differential</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive differential market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Open Differential** allows wheels to rotate at different speeds, ideal for straight-line driving but can cause traction issues on slippery surfaces. </p><p>2. **Lock Differential** ensures both wheels turn at the same speed, improving traction in off-road conditions. </p><p>3. **Limited Slip Differential provides a balance, transmitting power to the wheel with better grip while allowing some speed differentiation. </p><p>4. **Electronic Differential** uses sensors and actuators for real-time adjustments, enhancing control and performance in diverse driving scenarios.</p></p>
