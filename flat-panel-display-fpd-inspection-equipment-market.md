<p><p>The Flat Panel Display (FPD) inspection equipment market is segmented into three main types: Array Test, Cell Test, and Module Test. Array Test focuses on assessing the integrity and performance of individual display arrays, ensuring uniform pixel functionality. Cell Test involves inspecting the individual display cells for defects and color accuracy. Module Test evaluates the complete assembly of the display module, including backlighting and interfaces, ensuring overall product quality and performance before final integration. Each type is crucial for maintaining display quality.</p></p>
